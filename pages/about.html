about

Füge Demirok is graduated from Yeditepe University, Social Sciences Institute, Plastic Arts and Design proficiency program, PhD., with distinction grade in 2022. Her doctoral thesis is on Individual Healing Tendencies in Participatory Art Practices. In 2016, she successfully completed the Arts in Healthcare certificate program, Faculty of Fine Arts, University of Florida and received her master’s degree in Arts and Design from Istanbul Kemerburgaz University, in June 2017. Her dissertation is on The Benefits and Applications of Art in the Healthcare Sector. In addition to her continuing works on painting, etching and sculpture, she gives lectures on how to improve the quality of time spent in health institutions by using Arts. The artist is a member of UPSD.
